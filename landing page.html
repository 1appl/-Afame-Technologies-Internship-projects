Project

Title : GLAUCOMA DETECTION USING CNN MODEL

Glaucoma is a condition that affects how well the human eye can see. This illness, which causes eyesight degeneration, is regarded as an irreversible condition. One of the most prevalent metabolic disorders (DMs), diabetes mellitus affects healthy living globally and can have mild to severe secondary consequences. Diabetic retinopathy (DR), which harms the retina and may result in vision loss, is one of the secondary consequences of DM. For the correct identification of glaucoma and diabetic retinopathy, many deep learning (DL) models have so far been created. The convolutional neural network (CNN) is used in the paper to offer an architecture for accurate glaucoma and diabetic retinopathy detection based on machine learning. The CNN model trained in the project achieved a accuracy of 92% on the test set indicating effectiveness of detecting eye diseases.
